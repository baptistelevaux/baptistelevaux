This website is dedicated to typography, in its form, creation and use. All typos were created in the workshop by people with intellectual disabilities. Typography is freely available.

This normographer was conceived by Teresa, a 20-year-old Liège artist, physically and mentally disabled since birth.

By purchasing this item, you also receive a booklet explaining the process and presenting the artists. Some of the profits collected are donated to the artist and the rest is used to finance material useful to the project and workshops.
